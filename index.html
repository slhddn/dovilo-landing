What is DoVille App?

DoVille App is a gamified to-do list and productivity app that transforms your daily tasks into an isometric city builder. Unlike traditional task managers, DoVille App rewards your productivity — every completed task and focus session earns coins that you can spend on buildings, decorations, and upgrades for your virtual city.

Three Pillars, One App

DoVille App brings together a full-featured to-do list, a Pomodoro-style focus timer, and a city building game into one seamless experience. Most productivity tools stop at checklists — DoVille App goes further by turning your progress into a growing city skyline with offices, houses, markets, parks, and more.

Key Features

  • Smart To-Do List — Create tasks with due dates, priorities, stars, and categories. Full task management with no limits on the free tier.
  • Pomodoro Focus Timer — Deep work sessions with customizable durations. Stay concentrated and earn rewards for every minute of focus.
  • Isometric City Builder — Watch your city grow as you complete tasks. Build houses, offices, markets, restaurants, parks, and roads on a beautiful isometric grid.
  • Reward Market — Spend earned coins on 50+ buildings, decorations, and city upgrades. Every building is a trophy of your hard work.
  • Dark & Light Themes — Beautiful teal-cyan design that adapts to your preference. Easy on the eyes for late-night study sessions.
  • 5 Languages — English, Turkish, Spanish, French, and German. Full localization for a global audience.
  • Offline-First — Works without internet. Your data is stored locally. Optional cloud sync for Pro users.
  • No Ads — DoVille App is completely ad-free, even on the free tier.

Who is DoVille App for?

DoVille App is perfect for students who need a fun study companion, professionals who want to gamify their workflow, freelancers managing multiple projects, and anyone who struggles with procrastination or staying focused. If you've been looking for a productivity app that feels rewarding rather than repetitive, DoVille App is designed for you.

Pricing

DoVille App is free to download and use. The free tier includes full task management, unlimited focus sessions, basic city building, and zero ads. DoVille Pro unlocks cloud sync, all buildings and decorations, advanced statistics, custom themes, and priority support — available as Monthly ($2.49), Yearly ($19.99, best value), or Lifetime ($49.99).

If you choose to enable cloud sync, we use Firebase Authentication to verify your identity via Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In. In this case, the following data is stored securely on Firebase servers:

— Your task and city progress data
