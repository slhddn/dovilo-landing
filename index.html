FAQ

Common questions

What is Dovilo?

Dovilo is a gamified productivity app that combines a to-do list, a Pomodoro-style focus timer, and an isometric city builder. Complete your tasks and focus sessions to earn bricks, then build houses, offices, parks, and more in your own virtual city.

Is Dovilo free?

Yes! The free tier includes full to-do management, unlimited focus sessions, basic city building, dark & light themes, and zero ads. Pro unlocks cloud sync, all buildings, advanced statistics, and more.

Does Dovilo work offline?

Yes! Dovilo is offline-first. All your tasks, focus sessions, and city progress are stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is an optional Pro feature for keeping data across multiple devices.

Does Dovilo have ads?

No. Dovilo is completely ad-free, both in the free tier and in Pro. We believe ads break focus, which is the opposite of what a productivity app should do.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. To cancel, go to your device's subscription settings. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

What is Dovilo?

Dovilo is a gamified to-do list and productivity app that transforms your daily tasks into an isometric city builder. Unlike traditional task managers, Dovilo rewards your productivity — every completed task and focus session earns coins that you can spend on buildings, decorations, and upgrades for your virtual city.

Three Pillars, One App

Dovilo brings together a full-featured to-do list, a Pomodoro-style focus timer, and a city building game into one seamless experience. Most productivity tools stop at checklists — Dovilo goes further by turning your progress into a growing city skyline with offices, houses, markets, parks, and more.

Key Features

  • Smart To-Do List — Create tasks with due dates, priorities, stars, and categories. Full task management with no limits on the free tier.
  • Pomodoro Focus Timer — Deep work sessions with customizable durations. Stay concentrated and earn rewards for every minute of focus.
  • Isometric City Builder — Watch your city grow as you complete tasks. Build houses, offices, markets, restaurants, parks, and roads on a beautiful isometric grid.
  • Reward Market — Spend earned coins on 50+ buildings, decorations, and city upgrades. Every building is a trophy of your hard work.
  • Dark & Light Themes — Beautiful navy-amber design that adapts to your preference. Easy on the eyes for late-night study sessions.
  • 5 Languages — English, Turkish, Spanish, French, and German. Full localization for a global audience.
  • Offline-First — Works without internet. Your data is stored locally. Optional cloud sync for Pro users.
  • No Ads — Dovilo is completely ad-free, even on the free tier.

Who is Dovilo for?

Dovilo is perfect for students who need a fun study companion, professionals who want to gamify their workflow, freelancers managing multiple projects, and anyone who struggles with procrastination or staying focused. If you've been looking for a productivity app that feels rewarding rather than repetitive, Dovilo is designed for you.

Pricing

Dovilo is free to download and use. The free tier includes full task management, unlimited focus sessions, basic city building, and zero ads. Dovilo Pro unlocks cloud sync, desktop app access (Windows & Mac), all buildings and decorations, advanced statistics, custom themes, and priority support — available as Monthly ($2.49), Yearly ($19.99, best value), or Lifetime ($49.99).

The Philosophy of Dovilo

Dovilo isn't just another to-do list; it's a living blueprint of your achievements. We believe that productivity shouldn't feel like a chore — it should feel like progress you can see.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between abstract goals and tangible results. By turning your focus hours into bricks and your completed tasks into skyscrapers, we provide a visual sanctuary for your hard work. Whether you are a student, a creative, or a professional, Dovilo helps you step into the "Mayor's Office" of your life and build the future you've always envisioned, one task at a time.
